GYMKHANA NIT Agartala

NIT Agartala is one of the most prestiguos instititute of India. It was founded as Tripura Engineering College in 1965 and declared a National Institute of Technology (NIT) in 2006.

The National Institute of Technology, Agartala, shortened to NIT Agartala or NITA, is an institute of national importance. Having it’s campus situated in lush green complex ,this institute have earned respect for it’s academic excellence and co curricular dominance.

Along with offering four year undergraduate courses in various streams of engineering,this institution also provides dual degree,master degree and phD courses . With the support of professors and all non teaching staffs along with the enthusiasm of students,this institute sets an example in the process of learning.
